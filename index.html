About Us

Badra Studio is a professional illustration studio based in Indonesia. We specialise in crafting compelling visual narratives across editorial, advertising, publishing and digital media. Our work ranges from concept art and character design to detailed illustrations that tell a story and inspire imagination.

Founded by a passionate team of artists, our design philosophy is grounded in minimalism — utilising clean lines and striking contrasts to convey complex ideas simply and beautifully. We collaborate with clients around the globe to bring ideas to life and embrace each project as a unique opportunity to push creative boundaries.
